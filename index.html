About IJMART

Our Mission

The International Journal of Multidisciplinary Advanced Research Trends (IJMART) is a peer-reviewed journal that publishes original research across various disciplines. It aims to provide a platform for researchers, engineers, scientists, and educators from all fields to share their findings and contribute to global knowledge. The journal emphasizes originality, timeliness, relevance, and readability in its publications.

Publication Ethics

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of publication ethics and follow strict guidelines to ensure the integrity of all published work. Our peer-review process ensures quality and originality in all published papers.

Impact Factor

IJMART has consistently maintained a high impact factor, reflecting the quality and influence of the research papers published in our journal. We are indexed in major academic databases and citation indices.

Open Access Policy

We believe in making research freely accessible to all. Our open access policy ensures that all published articles are immediately and permanently free for everyone to read and download.
